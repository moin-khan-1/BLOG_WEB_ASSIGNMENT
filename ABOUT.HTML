More Information:-

  • The iPhone 17 Series brings together four powerful models iPhone 17, iPhone 17 Air, iPhone 17 Pro, and iPhone 17 Pro Max each designed to deliver next level performance and style.
  • All models feature Super Retina XDR OLED displays with ProMotion 120 Hz refresh rate, the new A19 chip for faster performance and enhanced AI processing, and upgraded 48 MP camera systems for stunning photos and videos. The standard Phone 17 offers balanced power and value, while the iPhone 17 Air focuses on ultra-thin, lightweight elegance. The Pro model adds a triple-lens setup with superior zoom and build quality, and the Pro Max takes it to the max with a larger 6.9-inch display, bigger battery, and the most advanced camera technology yet.
